About the studio

Made by one hand, with care.

Britt Built is a one-person digital goods studio rooted in the belief that good design should be accessible. Every file is crafted with real use in mind — not just what looks good in a mockup, but what actually works in your daily life.

Each product is tested, refined, and designed to be immediately useful from the moment you download it. No subscriptions, no logins — just well-made files that are yours to keep.
